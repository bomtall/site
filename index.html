Data Analyst

After being a successful and self-employed painter and decorator for 12 years in North Yorkshire, I returned to education as a mature student and achieved A-Levels in Computer Science & Mathematics. I then applied to a degree apprenticeship opportunity with Skanska UK and moved to London. With this opportunity I have been able to apply many of my skills to Construction which is a complex industry going through a period of intense digitalisation. I have been mainly focused on automating data collection, storage and presentation using Python and Power BI. I also developed some applications for the business using Microsoft PowerApps and Azure SQL databases. These applications have now been deployed to the IT production environemnt and are used by staff internally. I have had the opportunity to present my work and analysis to senior leadership teams within the business with fantastic feedback and was awarded Trainee of The Year for Skanska UK. After one year on the trainee scheme, I have been promoted early to a Data Analyst position.

At university I have furthered my technical skills by building a statistics library in C, task management software in Python and a website to showcase my work and CV. I am interested in mathematics, analysis and machine learning but also cloud computing and data architecture. I have scored very highly for my work so far which also included writing an academic literature review on graph databases and property data analysis. I am highly motivated, keen to advance my learning and enjoy working as a team. I particularly enjoy writing code and I took the opportunity to attend EuroPython in 2022 where I became a contributor to open source libraries for the first time.
